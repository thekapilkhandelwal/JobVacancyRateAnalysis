Job Vacancy Rate by Industry and Occupational Group

Visualize and analyse Job Vacancy Rate which is the total number of job vacancies divided by the total demand for labour at the end of the quarter. The total demand for labour is defined as the sum of the number of employees and job vacancies at the end of the quarter.
